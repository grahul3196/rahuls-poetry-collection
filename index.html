My Poems

The Morning Sun

The morning sun rises high,
With a golden hue in the sky.
Birds chirp and sing with glee,
A beautiful day for you and me.

A Silent Night

The night is silent, the stars are bright,
The moon shines with a gentle light.
In the stillness, dreams take flight,
A peaceful end to a tranquil night.

Nature's Beauty

The beauty of nature is all around,
In the trees, the flowers, the ground.
A symphony of colors and sounds,
In this paradise, peace is found.
